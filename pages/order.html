How to Order Your Cake

Thank you for choosing Sweet Bloom. Each cake is handmade to order and designed especially for your celebration.

How to Place an Order

To order a your custom cake, please fill in the order form on this page or contact us via email. When ordering, please include the date of the event, number of servings, preferred flavor, and design ideas.

All cakes are made to order and priced individually depending on size, flavor, decoration, and complexity of the design.

Ordering Time

We recommend placing your order at least:

  • 3–4 weeks in advance for wedding cakes
  • 1–2 weeks in advance for celebration cakes
  • 5–7 days in advance for bento cakes

Last-minute orders may be accepted depending on availability.

Payment

A 20% deposit is required to confirm your order. The remaining balance must be paid before collection or delivery.

Payments can be made via bank transfer, card payment, or cash.

Please note that your order is not confirmed until the deposit has been received.

Allergy Information

Our cakes are made in a kitchen that handles the following allergens: Gluten, Eggs, Milk, Nuts, Peanuts, Soy, and Sesame.

If you have any allergies or dietary requirements, please inform us when placing your order.
